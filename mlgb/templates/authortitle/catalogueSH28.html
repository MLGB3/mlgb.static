Collections with this common title invariably begin with Rufinus's translation (often ascribed to Jerome) of the Historia monachorum in Aegypto (PL 21. 387–462; ed. E. Jerome's Lives of St Paul the Hermit, St Hilarion, and St Malchus (CPL 617–19) also make a frequent appearance.
