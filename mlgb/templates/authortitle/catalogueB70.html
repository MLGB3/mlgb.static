  • B70.2: John Dumbleton [†1349]
    Summa logicae et naturalis philosophiae unpr.; Sharpe, Latin Writers, 239.

    Compendium seu Lilium medicinae pr. Lyon 1510; Sharpe, Latin Writers, 144; Thorndike/Kibre 3, 881.
