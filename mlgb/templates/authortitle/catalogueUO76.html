UO76. List of books recorded by an Italian visitor to Oxford, early 16th cent.

15 identified entries found.
  • UO76.1 (`B. Greg. speculum 3 libri'): Adalbert of Metz [† c. 980]
    CMA Gallia 1/1. 13–15.
    Speculum Gregorii preface pr. PL 136. 1309–12; chapter headings, ed. R. Wasselynck, RTAM 34 (1967) 255–62; Stegmüller Bibl. 859; Bloomfield 3297.
  • UO76.2 (`Gullielmi Autissiodoren' super ep. Pauli'): Haimo of Auxerre [fl. 840–865]
    Textes et manuscrits exégétiques carolingiens. Études autour d'Haymon d'Auxerre, ed. S. Shimahara (Turnhout 2007).
    Commentary on the Pauline Epistles pr. Strassburg 1519, &c.; PL 117. 361–938; Stegmüller Bibl. 3101–114.
  • UO76.3: Robert Grosseteste, known as Lincolniensis [†1253], bishop of Lincoln
    writings and speeches at the papal curia, Lyon 1250, as collected by Robert Marsh ed. S. Gieben in Collectanea Franciscana 41 (1971) 340–93; Thomson, Grosseteste, 141–7.
  • UO76.4 (`Hugonis de S. V. chronica'): Hugh of Saint-Victor OSA [c1096–1141]
    Chronica de tribus maximis circumstantiis gestorum unpr.; prologue, ed. W. M. Green, Speculum 18 (1943) 484–93 (text, 488–92); Goy, 36–43. [J. Harrison, `The English reception of Hugh of Saint-Victor's Chronica', eBLJ (2002).]
  • UO76.5a (`Ghilde historia'): Gildas [early 6th cent.]
    De excidio Britanniae CPL 1319; ed. T. Mommsen, MGH Auct. Antiq. 13 (1898), 25–85; ed. M. Winterbottom (Chichester 1978).
  • UO76.5b (`de regi Cuntone'): Encomium Emmae Reginae ed. A. Campbell, Camd. 3rd ser. 72 (1949).
  • UO76.6 (`epistola Samuelis israelite de longa captiuitate iudeorum'): Samuel Israelita []
    Epistola contra iudaeorum errores, tr. Alphonsus Boni Hominis pr. [Sant' Orso 1474] (Bod-Inc S046), &c.
  • UO76.7 = UO77.26: Q. Curtius Rufus [1st cent.]
    Historia Alexandri pr. [Venice c. 1471] (GW 7871), &c.; ed. K. Müller & H. Schönfeld (Munich 1954). [For anonymous entries, see also Historia Alexandri magni.]
  • UO76.9 (`Egidius de naturis motu et significatione cometarum'): Gilles de Lessines OP [† after 1304]
    DSB 5. 401–402.
    De essentia motu et significatione cometarum ed. L. Thorndike, Latin Treatises on Comets (Chicago, IL, 1950), 103–184; Thorndike/Kibre 1286, 1527; Kaeppeli 44; Weijers, 2. 63.
  • UO76.10 (`Alberti Magni de xij experimentis de corio serpentis'): Ioannes Paulinus []
    De corio serpentis ed. J. W. S. Johnson, Bulletin de la Société française de l'histoire de médicine 12 (1913) 257–67; Thorndike/Kibre 295. The copy in All Souls College, MS 81, ends, `et sic expliciunt .12. experimenta naturalia de spolio serpentis'.
  • UO76.11 (`Rogerii Bachonis aggregatorium totius artis geomantie ad astrologiam reducte', inc.): Rolandus Scriptoris []
    Compilatorium artis geomantiae, inc. `Artem geomancie sub breuibus et claris uerbis compilandam intende' unpr.; Thorndike/Kibre 146. There is a copy in BL MS Royal 12 C. xvi.
  • UO76.12: Robert Grosseteste, known as Lincolniensis [†1253], bishop of Lincoln
    Computus correctorius ed. R. Steele, Opera hactenus inedita Rogeri Baconis (Oxford 1909–40), 6. 212–67; Thomson, Grosseteste, 95–6; Sharpe, Latin Writers, 540.
  • UO76.13a (`Petrus de Lene de S. Audomaro tractatus de nouo quadrante'): Petrus de S. Audomaro [fl. 1293]
    Nouus quadrans correctus, building on the recent work of Profatius Iudaeus ed. F. Saaby Pedersen, Corpus philosophorum Danicorum 10 (1983–4), 2. 589–648; Thorndike/Kibre 1267.
  • UO76.13b (`eiusdem instrumentum menodia . .'): Petrus de S. Audomaro [fl. 1293]
    Tractatus semissae ed. F. Saaby Pedersen, Corpus philosophorum Danicorum medii aevi 10 (1983–4), 2. 683–729; Thorndike/Kibre 1288. The last chapter of the work (Thorndike/Kibre 295) has been treated separately in BA1.1148.
  • UO76.14 (`Petri de Palude de potestate ecclesiastica') = UO77.25: Guillaume de Pierre Godin OP [†1336]
    De causa immediata ecclesiasticae potestatis ed. W. D. McCready (Toronto 1982); Glorieux Rép. 59j; Kaeppeli 1632.
