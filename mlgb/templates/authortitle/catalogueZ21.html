  • Z21.18: Aelred of Rievaulx OCist [1109–1167], abbot of Rievaulx

  • Z21.19: Haimo of Auxerre [fl. 840–865]
    Textes et manuscrits exégétiques carolingiens. Études autour d'Haymon d'Auxerre, ed. S. Shimahara (Turnhout 2007).
    Commentary on the Song of Songs CPL 910; PL 70. 1055–1106 (as Cassiodorus, with biblical quotations marked); PL 117. 295–358 (as Haimo); Stegmüller Bibl. 3065, 3079.
  • Z21.20: Cassiodorus Senator [c485–580]
    [dub.] Commentary on Lamentations unidentified.
  • Z21.21: Robert of Cricklade OSA [† after 1188]
    Homiliae super Ezechielem unpr.; Schneyer Rep. 5. 171–6; Stegmüller Bibl. 7455.
  • Z21.22: Richard Fishacre OP [†1248]
    Super Deus illuminatio (Ps. 26) not known to survive; Sharpe, Latin Writers, 476; Stegmüller Bibl. 7268.
