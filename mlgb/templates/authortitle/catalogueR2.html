REGISTRUM ANGLIE: REGISTRUM ANGLIE

R2. Union catalogue by author and title, early 14th cent.

  • R2.†1: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    Decreta Gregorii seems most likely to refer to the `Decretum ad clerum in basilica beati Petri apostoli' (5 July 595) PL 77. 1334–9. [This is found alone, for example, in BL MS Royal 6 A. XIII; the imperial dating clause is in the opening sentence.]
  • R2.2: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    Homiliae XL in euangelia CPL 1711; pr. [Augsburg] 1473 (GW 11418), &c.
  • R2.3: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    De cura pastorali CPL 1712.
  • R2.4: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    Dialogi CPL 1713; ed. A. de Vogüé, SChr 251, 260, 265 (1978–80).
  • R2.5: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    Moralia in Iob CPL 1708; ed. M. Adriaen, CCSL 143, 143A, 143B (1979–85).
  • R2.6: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    Homiliae in Ezechielem CPL 1710; ed. M. Adriaen, CCSL 142 (1971).
  • R2.7: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    Registrum CPL 1714; ed. D. Norberg, CCSL 140, 140A (1982).
  • R2.8: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    Libellus responsionum, a compilation which circulated separately in penitential collections as well as in Bede's Historia ecclesiastica I 27 M. Deanesly & P. Grosjean in JEH 10 (1959) 1–49; P. Meyvaert in England before the Conquest. Studies presented to Dorothy Whitelock (Cambridge 1971), 15–33; CPL 1327.
  • R2.9: Ambrosius Autpertus [†781]
    De conflictu uitiorum et uirtutum PL 17. 1057–74 & under other authors; ed. R. Weber, CCCM 27B (1979) 907–31; Bloomfield 0455.
  • R2.†10: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    Homiliae in Canticum canticorum CPL 1709; ed. P. P. Verbraken, CCSL 144 (1963) 1–46. [See also Robert de Tumbalena (ps. Gregory), Commentary on the Song of Songs.]
  • R2.11: Adalbert of Metz [† c. 980]
    CMA Gallia 1/1. 13–15.
    Speculum Gregorii preface pr. PL 136. 1309–12; chapter headings, ed. R. Wasselynck, RTAM 34 (1967) 255–62; Stegmüller Bibl. 859; Bloomfield 3297.
  • R2.12: Gregory of Nazianzus [330–390]
    De reconciliacione monachorum (or. 7) ed. A. Engelbrecht, CSEL 46 (1910) 209–33.
  • R2.13: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    [app.] Excerpta librorum beati Gregorii, a collection of excerpts arranged in sixteen books and 317 chapters, which survives in several manuscripts. See notes.
  • R2.14: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    [app.] De inuentione librorum Moralium S. Gregorii, usually prefixed to copies of the Moralia in Iob PL 75. 507–510; BHL 3647.
  • R2.15: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    [dub.] `Super Genesim' unidentified.
  • R2.†16: John the Deacon of Rome [late 9th cent.]
    Vita S. Gregorii papae PL 75. 59–242; BHL 3641–2.
  • R2.17: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    [pseud.] Symbolum fidei dictatum a beato Gregorio, often prefixed to Gregory's Register PL 77. 1327–9.
  • R2.18: Gregory the Great [c540–604, sedit 590–604]
    [dub.] `Liber sacramentorum' unidentified.
