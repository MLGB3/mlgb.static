UO42. List of books recorded by an Italian visitor to Oxford, early 16th cent.

16 identified entries found.

  • UO42.*2 (`descriptio locorum que uidit Bernardus sapiens quando iuit in Ierusalem'): Bernardus Monachus [9th cent.]

  • UO42.*2x: Baldwin of Ford OCist [†1190], archbishop of Canterbury

  • UO42.3: Sozomenus Pistoriensis [1387–1458]
    Chronicon uniuersale [to 1456] part ed. G. Zaccagnin, Rerum italicarum scriptores new ser. 16/1 (1908).
  • UO42.*4: Iohannes Mansionarius [early 14th cent.]
    Breuis adnotatio de duobus Pliniis ed. C. Cipolla in Miscellanea Ceriani (Milan 1910), 758–63; E. T. Merill in Classical Philology 5 (1910) 186–8.
  • UO42.*5a (`Cicero ad Quintum fratrem de petitione consulatus'): Q Tullius Cicero [102–43 BC]
    [pseud.] Commentariolum petitionis ed. D. Nardo (Milan 1972). Treated as a work of M. Tullius Cicero though the text is addressed to him by his brother Quintus.
  • UO42.*5b (`eiusdem de creatione celi et mundi et eorum eternitate'): M. Tullius Cicero [106–43 BC]
    Somnium Scipionis (De re publica VI 9–29) ed. K. Ziegler, Teubner (1969), 126–36.
  • UO42.*5c: M. Tullius Cicero [106–43 BC]
    De haruspicum responsis ed. T. Maslowski, Teubner (1981).
  • UO42.*6a: M. Terentius Varro [116–27 BC]
    De lingua Latina pr. [Rome about 1470/71] (BSB-Ink V55), &c.; ed. G. Goetz & F. Schoell, Teubner (1910).
  • UO42.*6b (`Messallae oraciones ad Octauianum Augustum de progenie sue et regionibus urbis Romae'): Ps. Messalla, De progenie Caesaris Augusti pr. with the works of Florus, Basel 1532 (Adams F642), 116–26, &c.
  • UO42.*6x: Andreas Floccus (Andrea Domenico Fiocco) [c1400–1452]
    (attrib.), De Romanis magistratibus pr. Venice 1474 (`Lucius Fenestella', GW 10038), &c.
