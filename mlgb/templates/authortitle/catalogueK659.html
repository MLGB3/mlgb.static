  • K659: `Walter of Exeter'
    Britannorum chronica a ghost, created by Kirkstead from a copy of Higden's Polychronicon that read `Walterus Exoniensis archidiaconus' (I 2, ed. Lumby, p. 24); Sharpe, Latin Writers, 734. The author in question is Walter Map, archdeacon of Oxford.
