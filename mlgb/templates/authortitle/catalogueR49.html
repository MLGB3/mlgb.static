REGISTRUM ANGLIE: REGISTRUM ANGLIE

R49. Union catalogue by author and title, early 14th cent.

  • R49.1: Berengaudus [9th cent.], monk of Ferrières
    [dub.] `De incarnacione uerbi' unidentified.
  • R49.2: Berengaudus [9th cent.], monk of Ferrières
    Expositio super septem uisiones libri Apocalypsis pr. as the work of Ambrose, Paris 1554, &c.; PL 17. 765–970 (with the works of Ambrose); Stegmüller Bibl. 1711.
  • R49.3: Berengar of Tours [c1010–1088]
    Epistolae, did not normally circulate as a collection, though one such was edited from a 16th-cent. copy, C. Erdmann & N. Fickermann, Brief-sammlungen der Zeit Heinrichs IV, MGH Briefen 5 (1950), 132–72.
  • R49.4: Haimo of Auxerre [fl. 840–865]
    Textes et manuscrits exégétiques carolingiens. Études autour d'Haymon d'Auxerre, ed. S. Shimahara (Turnhout 2007).
    Commentary on the Song of Songs CPL 910; PL 70. 1055–1106 (as Cassiodorus, with biblical quotations marked); PL 117. 295–358 (as Haimo); Stegmüller Bibl. 3065, 3079.
