UNIVERSITY AND COLLEGE LIBRARIES OF CAMBRIDGE: Donor documents

UC134. Record of books purchased by Richard Holme, 1406; King's Hall

  • UC134.1: Iohannes Andreae (Giovanni d'Andrea) [c1270–1348]
    Nouellae in Decretales pr. Venice 1489 (GW 1729), &c.; pr. Venice 1581; Schulte, 2. 219–21. For the various recensions, see K. Pennington in ZRG kan. 74 (1988) 328–47.
  • UC134.3: Liber sextus Decretalium, continuing the Decretals from Gregory IX (1234) to Boniface VIII (1298) pr. Strassburg 1465 (GW 4848), &c.; ed. E. Friedberg, Corpus iuris canonici, 2. 933–1124; Schulte, 2. 34–44. [The ordinary gloss on the Sext is that of Iohannes Andreae; at Paris that of Iohannes Monachus was preferred; the triple gloss also contained Guido de Baysio.]
