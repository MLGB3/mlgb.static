HENRY DE KIRKESTEDE: HENRY DE KIRKESTEDE

K215. Catalogus, c. 1360

  • K215.1: Gilbert de la Porrée [1076–1154]
    Gloss on the Psalms (Media glosatura) unpr.; Stegmüller Bibl. 2511.
  • K215.2: Gilbert the Universal [†1134], bishop of London
    (attrib.), Gloss on the Old Testament. Gilbert wrote the Glossa ordinaria on Lamentations, and very probably that on the Pentateuch and the major prophets; Robert of Bridlington also attributed to him the gloss on the minor prophets. The extant manuscript contains the glosses from Genesis to Kings.
