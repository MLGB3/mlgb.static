AUGUSTINIAN CANONS: Thornton

A34. Lincolnshire list, 1528

  • A34.1: Ivo of Chartres [c1040–1116]
    Panormia PL 161. 1041–1344.
  • A34.†2: Consuetudines et iustitiae (1091) ed. C. H. Haskins, Norman Institutions (Cambridge, MA, 1918), 281–4. [No English copies are known to survive.]
  • A34.3: William of Abingdon OP [† after 1244]
    Tractatus de septem uitiis not known to survive; Sharpe, Latin Writers, 745; Kaeppeli 1421.
  • A34.*4: William of Malmesbury OSB [c1080–1143]
    Gesta pontificum Anglorum ed. N. E. S. A. Hamilton, RS 52 (1870); ed. M. Winterbottom & R. M. Thomson, OMT (2007).
  • A34.*4x: Caesarius of Arles [c470–542], archbishop of Arles
    De igne purgatorio (serm. 179; ps. Augustine serm. 104) CPPM 1. 889; ed. G. Morin, CCSL 104 (1953) 724–9.
  • A34.*4x: Elias of Evesham OSB [fl. 1200]
    Quadrilogus de uita S. Thomae Cantuariensis ed. J. C. Robertson, RS 67/4 (1879), 266–424; BHL 8194–5.
  • A34.*4x: Eustace of Faversham OSB [† after 1244]
    (attrib.), Vita S. Edmundi Cantuariensis ed. C. H. Lawrence, St Edmund of Abingdon (Oxford 1960), 203–221; BHL 2412d.
  • A34.*4x: Gregory of Tours [538/9–594], bishop of Tours
    Miracula S. Andreae CPL 1027; ed. M. Bonnet, MGH Scr. rerum Merov. 1 (1937–51), 826–46; BHL 430.
  • A34.*4x: Innocent III (Lotharius de Segnis) [1160/61–1216, sedit 1198–1216]
    De missarum mysteriis PL 217. 773–916. [List of manuscripts by D. F. Wright in Rivista di storia della chiesa in Italia 29 (1975) 444–52; edition by C. Egger in progress.]
  • A34.*4x: Leontius [fl. 650], bishop of Neapolis (Cyprus)
    Vita S. Iohannis Eleemosynarii, tr. Anastasius Bibliothecarius PL 73. 337–84; BHL 4388.
  • A34.*4x: Robert Grosseteste, known as Lincolniensis [†1253], bishop of Lincoln
    [pseud.] De auersione a summo bono unpr.; Bloomfield 4190; Thomson, Grosseteste, 235–6.
  • A34.*4x: Robert Grosseteste, known as Lincolniensis [†1253], bishop of Lincoln
    [pseud.] De effectibus uirtutum unpr.; Bloomfield 3210, 3213; Thomson, Grosseteste, 236.
  • A34.*5: Petrus de Vinea [†1249], chancellor to Frederick II
    Summa dictaminis ed. S. Schardius, Epistolarum Petri de Vineis libri VI (Basel 1566); manuscripts listed by H. M. Schaller, Deutsches Archiv 12 (1956) 114–59.
