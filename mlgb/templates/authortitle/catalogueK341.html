HENRY DE KIRKESTEDE: HENRY DE KIRKESTEDE

K341. Catalogus, c. 1360

  • K341.*1: John of Tynemouth [mid 14th cent.], hagiographer
    Historia aurea unpr.; Sharpe, Latin Writers, 334.
  • K341.2: John of Tynemouth [mid 14th cent.], hagiographer
    Martyrologium, surviving only as extracts at the back of the Bury version of the Historia aurea.
  • K341.3: John of Tynemouth [mid 14th cent.], hagiographer
    Sanctilogium Angliae Walliae Scotiae Hiberniae unpr. as such but the contents largely printed by C. Horstman, Nova Legenda Anglie (Oxford 1901); Sharpe, Latin Writers, 334.
  • K341.5: John of Tynemouth [mid 14th cent.], hagiographer
    Expositiones morales allegoricae litterales not known to survive.
  • K341.6: John of Tynemouth [mid 14th cent.], hagiographer
    Lectionarium not known to survive.
