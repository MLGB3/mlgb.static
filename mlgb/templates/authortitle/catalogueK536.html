HENRY DE KIRKESTEDE: HENRY DE KIRKESTEDE

K536. Catalogus, c. 1360

  • K536: <>Severianus [† after 408], bishop of Gabala
    Homiliae Latin homilies are attributed to him in the Homiliary of Paul the Deacon. The sermon for Holy Innocents, inc. `Zelus quo tendat' (Paul the Deacon, I 34), travels elsewhere under the name of Augustine (CPPM 1. 1716), and has been edited among the sermons of Petrus Chrysologus (serm. 152), ed. A. Olivar, CCSL 24B (1982) 949–55.
